BRCA Foundation

Client: BRCA Foundation
Task: Build a visually stimulating site where people can learn about and contribute to funding cancer genetics research.

Overview

Acutulus was tasked with a ground up build for the brand new BRCA Foundation. We used modern techniques to bring the design to life.

Challenge

The BRCA Foundation was founded by a digital entrepreneur who knew having a visually stimulating and responsive website was key to boosting interest in research.

Strategy

Additionally the client had requested an admin side of the site so they could update content themselves as needed, so we developed a framework that allows their admins to publish changes on demand.

Result

A stunning page that can be iterated quickly and has expanded to exhibit the news, fund-raising events, and even podcasts created by the Foundation.
