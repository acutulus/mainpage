Meet.fm

Client: OSIX Inc
Task: Increase client's reliablity

Overview

Meet.fm is an online meeting platform that allows peer to peer meetings across iOS, Android, and desktop platforms. Meet.fm engaged Acutulus to supliment their in-house engineering team on key projects.

Challenge

As a fast-paced startup Meet.fm found itself with a growing need for specialist engineering talent to expand their meeting technology to a broad range of devices as well as migrate to a new video provider. However, hiring an in-house team for this task was not possible, and pulling the focus of its in-house team for this project would derail product growth.

Strategy

Meet.fm enlisted the help of Acutulus to work alongside thier in-house team to manage the expantion of the Meet.fm platform. The Acutulus engineers became an integrated part of the Meet.fm team, functioning within the Meet.fm company as full-time engineers for the extent of the project.

Result

The Meet.fm video provider migration was a success. In addition to the migration, Acutulus expanded the Meet.fm compatibility to iOS and adroid systems and continues to serve Meet.fm as mobile experts as needed. This ongoing partnership has allowed Meet.fm to focus on product development and company growth while having a dedicated team to ensure the platform was running optimally.
