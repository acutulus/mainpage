iTutor

Client: University of Warwick
Task: Build out vision

Overview

iTutor was the brainchild of Lecturer Nick Wake of the Warwick Business School. Wake understood the impact of technology, especially mobile phones, on the way his students learned. Wake dreamed of an custom application where lectures could easily upload practice questions to help students study on the go.

Challenge

While technology was widely used by both students and lecturer, the platform for interaction was not uniform. To be successful the application must be available to all students and easily used by lectures at all levels of technical proficiency.

Strategy

Wake tapped Acutulus to help this vision be realized. Acutulus worked with Wake through prototype, creation, and release of the application. To make iTutor useful to both lecturers and students Acutulus developed the application on multiple platforms with an easy-to-use UI that was consistent across all formats. A web application was developed for lecturers who required it to be easy to upload questions from the computer. A mobile application was developed across iOS and Android to allow students to utilize the resource regardless of device type.

Result

The iTutor application is now used across courses throughout the University of Warwick curriculum and has been adopted an official University application.
